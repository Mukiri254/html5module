Good Idea Flowerpots

We are committed to improving and taking care of our environment. By Recycling vehicle wheels that have come to their end of life cycle we REDUCE the amount of tyre rubber that end goes into land fill and into our water ways. We REUSE Those old wheels into more effective product that can continue to be functional even after their primary use has ended. We RECYCLE those old wheels into environmentally friendly garden planters/ containers/ FLOWERPOTS that can continue to grace our gardens and yards.

Working with schools

We have enabled local shools to participate in this noble cause and we continue to encourage people to recycle and reuse this very effective peice of engineering that will weather any storm and will last long/ HELPING THE ENVIRONMENT ONE WHEEL AT A TIME
